The Best Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe Ever Created

This just might be the best buffalo chicken dip recipe ever made by far. You thought the lasagna was good? Try this shit.

Ingredients

  • Chicken

    This buffalo chicken dip starts with two cans of chunk chicken, making it extra easy and affordable. Of course, you can substitute rotisserie chickem or shredded chicken breasts if you'd prefer

  • Cream Cheese

    Make sure the cream cheese is softened for easy incorporation.

  • Ranch Dressing

    A cup of ranch dressing adds creaminess and tang

  • Hot Sauce

    Hot pepper sauce (such as Frank's) turns up the heat, mimicking the spicy flavor of Buffalo chicken wings. Use more or less than the recipe calls for to suit your flavor preferences

  • Cheese

    Cheddar cheese is melted into the dip, creating a satisfyingly gooey texture and cheesy flavor

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Heat chicken and hot pepper sauce in a skilelt over medium heat until heated through, 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in cream cheese and ranch dressing. Cook and stir until well blended and warm, 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Mix in 1/2 of the Cheddar cheese , and transfer the mixture to a slow cooker. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over top.
  4. Cover and cook on low until dip is hot and bubbly, about 35 minutes.
  5. Serve with celery sticks and crackers
